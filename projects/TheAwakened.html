Description

This was the biggest project I have been a Lead Developer of. For this school project we had eight weeks with a group of nine people to make a 3D game based off a Myth. We had chosen the 'Dreamcatcher' myth and were about to make a Hack and Slash game where you had to collect souls in your Dreamcatcher. Controlled with an Xbox Controller.

I have learned a lot from this project. Such as how organized you should be with your code as it will get messy when multiple people work on it. Also how hard it is to get a large amount of people to work on the same game with the same Artstyle. We had a lot of performance issues when the art and lightings got imported because of the high quality.

In the end we managed to finalize our game with Cinematics and advanced lighting.

To close off our second year of our education, we had to present our game in front of a hundred people. There were eight people from different game company's which critizied our game and gave feedback which we used for our next projects.

Responsibilities

I was responsible for discussing with the Lead Artist and the project owner and giving tasks to the other developers, which was done every week in a professional manner.

Development wise, I was responsible a lot of different things, such as Camera Behaviour, Enemy spawning, Enviromental effects, User Interface, Importing every art asset and merging other developers work together as one game.
