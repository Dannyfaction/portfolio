ABOUT ME





Hi! I'm Danny, a Game Technologies bachelor student at the Breda University of Applied Sciences. I started my game development journey at the age of 18 at Mediacollege Amsterdam.

Over the years I amassed a great set of skills working with different commercial game engines for different platforms and created my own custom engine with a small team where I learned to work with various libraries.

During my studies at Breda I found myself in love with Custom Engine & Tools development and decided to make Tools Programming my specialization, working together with multi-disciplinary teams to make Tools for their needs.
