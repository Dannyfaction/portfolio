ABOUT ME





ALL-ROUNDGAME PROGRAMMER

My name is Danny, I recently graduated from Mediacollege Amsterdam where I studied Game Development for four years.

During this study, in my third and fourth year, I was an intern at Altergo Games, a Game Design studio and at Scopic, a Virtual Reality studio.
At the internships I worked client oriented where I developed games and apps in Unity. Some of the projects I have worked on can be viewed below with details about the project and my responsibilities during the project.
