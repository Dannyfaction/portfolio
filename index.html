About Me

My name is Danny Kruiswijk and I am currently studying Game Development on Mediacollege Amsterdam (2014 – 2018). I have a lot of experience with the Unity 3D engine and the C# coding language. I am able to work together in a group using the SCRUM method in order to make quality games. I have also been lead-developer multiple times in school projects which went really well.

I have worked with Oculus Virtual Reality, Xbox Kinect Movement Sensor and the Leap Motion hand movement tracking.

I am always open to learn new methods, coding languages, engines and more. My dream when I am done with school is to work at small, successful indie game companies where the atmosphere is subtle.
