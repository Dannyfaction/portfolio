About Me

My goal in life is to give people, especially children, as much fun playing games I developed as I had when I was a kid and playing games. My name is Danny Kruiswijk and I am currently following a Game Developer education at Mediacollege Amsterdam.

I am an all-round Game developer and I have been programming for 3 years now. In almost all of my projects I have worked with I have been the lead-developer because I thought that this very important for my progress. The projects went really well as you can see the end results above.

I program mainly in Unity C# but I also have experience with Actionscript 3 and JavaScript. Furthermore I also have experience with HTML and CSS.

I am able to apply some hardware in games, such as the Leap Motion hand movement tracking, the Xbox Kinect Movement Sensor and the Oculus Virtual Reality Headset. I have applied these in some of my projects which I have successfully completed.

When I program in C# I always try to do this object orientated and as efficiently as possible. I am good with using Git and the SCRUM work method and am able to apply this in practice.

I am eager to learning new programming languages, engines, work methods and programs on my own tempo.

My goal for the future is to work at indie game companies where the atmosphere is subtle and where you might build a good relationship with your colleagues.
